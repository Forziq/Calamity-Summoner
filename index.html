Pre-Boss / Early Game

Spawn → Desert Scourge / Eye of Cthulhu

Armor

  • Apprentice's Hat + Robe + Trousers

Weapons

  • Slime Staff

Accessories & Tips

  • Aglet • Anklet of the Wind • Summoning Potion
  • Build a simple platform arena and kite.

Pre-Evil Bosses

After Desert Scourge / EoC → Hive Mind / Perforators

Armor

  • Apprentice's Set or Bee Armor

Weapons

  • Early Calamity minions from evil biomes + basic whips

Accessories & Tips

  • Hermes Boots • Shield of Cthulhu • Summoning Potion
  • Focus on arena building before evil bosses.

Pre-Skeletron

After evil bosses

Armor

  • Apprentice's Set

Weapons

  • Spinal Tap + best available minion

Accessories & Tips

  • Summoner Emblem • Movement accessories
  • Dungeon loot helps a lot here.
4

Pre-Wall of Flesh

End of Pre-Hardmode

Armor

  • Apprentice's Set or early Calamity summon armor

Weapons

  • Strong pre-WoF minions + whips for tagging

Accessories & Tips

  • Full early summon accessories • Obsidian Skin Potion
  • Long bridge arena recommended.
5

Pre-Mechanical Bosses

Hardmode start (after WoF)

Armor

  • Dark Artist Set (buffed in 2.1)

Weapons

  • Post-WoF reworked Calamity summon weapons

Accessories & Tips

  • Avenger Emblem • Celestial Shell • Minion speed items
  • Mechanical bosses drop excellent summon materials.
6

Pre-Plantera

After all Mechanical Bosses

Armor

  • Hybrid summon sets from mech drops

Weapons

  • High-tier minions + sentinels

Accessories & Tips

  • Full summon damage / crit / speed optimization
  • Strong jungle arena is essential.
7

Pre-Golem

After Plantera

Armor

  • Stronger post-Plantera summon armor

Weapons

  • Golem-dropped or crafted high-damage minions

Accessories & Tips

  • Wings + expert accessories
  • Farm Lihzahrd Temple for potions and mounts.

Pre-Devourer of Gods / Pre-Yharon

Mid Post-ML

Armor

  • Top-tier summon sets (often hybrid mage/rogue pieces)

Weapons

  • Exo / Profaned-level summons

  • Full endgame loadout with max minion count + damage
  • Heavy arena + potion use recommended.
11

Pre-Supreme Calamitas & Endgame

Final stretch

Armor

  • Best post-SCal summon armor (full set bonuses for minion spam)

Weapons

  • Ultimate reworked summon weapons + sentinels

Accessories & Tips

  • Perfectly optimized (Menacing/Deadly modifiers + all buffs)
  • Summoner is S-tier for SCAL and beyond. Focus on dodging while minions carry.
